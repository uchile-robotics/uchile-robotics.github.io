The UChileHomebreakers Robotics Team is currently working on 3 robots: Bender, Pepper and Jaime. All teams work at the Robotics Laboratory of the Electrical Engineering Department of the University of Chile, and are supported by the Advance Mining Technology Center (AMTC).

UChile Robotics is lead by Dr. Javier Ruiz del Solar.
